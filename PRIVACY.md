# Privacy Policy for NY Times Connections Helper

*Last updated: August 2, 2025*

## Overview

NY Times Connections Helper is a browser extension designed to enhance your experience with the NY Times Connections game by providing color-coding and organization features. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how our extension works.

## Information We Collect

### Data Collection
- **No Personal Data**: This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you.
- **No Account Required**: The extension works without requiring any user accounts or login credentials.
- **No Analytics**: We do not use any analytics services or tracking tools.

### Local Storage
- **Game State Only**: The extension may temporarily store game-related information (such as card colors and positions) locally in your browser to maintain your preferences during gameplay.
- **No Server Storage**: All data remains on your device and is never sent to external servers.

## Permissions Explained

Our extension requests the following permissions:

### Context Menus Permission
- **Purpose**: To provide right-click menu options for game features
- **Usage**: Adds helpful menu items when right-clicking on game elements

### Host Permission (nytimes.com)
- **Purpose**: To function specifically on NY Times Connections game pages
- **Scope**: Limited to `https://www.nytimes.com/games/connections*`
- **Usage**: Required to inject our helper features into the game interface

## Data Usage

### What We Do
- Enhance the visual presentation of the Connections game
- Provide drag-and-drop functionality for game pieces
- Add color-coding features to help organize your thoughts
- Store your color preferences locally for the current game session

### What We Don't Do
- Access your browsing history
- Monitor your activity outside the Connections game
- Collect any personal information
- Share data with third parties
- Store data on external servers
- Track your gameplay statistics

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or external APIs. All functionality is contained within the extension itself.

## Data Security

- All extension data remains on your local device
- No network connections are made to external servers
- Your game preferences are stored only in your browser's local storage
- Data is automatically cleared when you close the browser or clear your browsing data

## Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The extension is designed to work with a word puzzle game and does not require any personal information from users of any age.

## Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the extension after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Open Source

This extension is open source, and you can review the complete source code to verify our privacy practices. The code is available in our repository and contains no hidden functionality.

## Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the extension's data practices, please:

- Review the source code in this repository
- Open an issue on our GitHub repository
- The extension operates entirely locally with no data collection, so there are no privacy concerns to report

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data:
- There is no personal data to access, modify, or delete
- No data is shared with third parties
- All extension data is under your control through your browser settings

You can disable or remove the extension at any time through your browser's extension management interface.
